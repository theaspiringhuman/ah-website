**The passive observer lives out his adventures through a screen as his life passes him by.**

He was born for heroism and danger,

but he has gradually lost his adventurous faculties.

He has been rendered immobile,

become docile and tame,

ever longing for his next virtual fix.

A hero for a moment in his own mind.

King of nothing and no one.

**He is the perfect citizen.**
