Our love was...

Like a fly

Tied to a string

Held by some little boy

Who will dirty his knees

When he falls

And we fall

Never free at all

Were we?


Because...

Fate plays its game

And we ain't got no say

We just go our own way

To stay safe

Are we safe?

Is this the way?

  
Heavenly...

What you said to me

Was my name for a day

Suddenly

Maybe gradually

We grew further away

From heaven

Was it heaven?


Or hell?
