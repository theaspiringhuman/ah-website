Like a deeply rooted oak tree
kindness is a constant
Though the wind & rain should hurl their threats
& rattle her branches
She remains calm & steady
Down go her roots into the earth
& up she lifts her head into the sky
Seasons pass through her & strengthen her substance

Many species of life rise & fall all around her
Each one a witness to & a beneficiary of her compassionate care
Some would grow up around her body & threaten to consume her 
to take for themselves her substance
(but can kindness be received in this way?)

Abundantly she gives 
& in the giving 
she quenches an infinite thirst
No, you cannot exhaust or consume kindness
She is a constant
Eternally rooted
in the soil of [[love]]
