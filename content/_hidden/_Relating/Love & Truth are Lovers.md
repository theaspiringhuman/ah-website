I believe that [[truth]] & the essence of all things really, is much simpler than we tend to make it out to be. In fact, I believe simplicity is an essential feature on the path to [[truth]]. 

There's a sort of stripping down & refining process that has to take place before we can see clearly. A purging of the superfluousness of our vision. I believe also that humility is an absolute requirement on the path to truth. 

Pride blinds us & causes us to stumble all over the place. 
& above all, we must walk with [[love]] in our hearts. [[Love]] is the key. 

Magnetism draws like to like, & the [[truth]] is indivisible from [[love]]. The two are lovers who seek always after union.
[[Truth]] welcomes with open arms & a passionate longing the [[love]] that is in you.
