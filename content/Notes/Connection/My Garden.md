I feel a constant call to be more attentive to my garden of relationships.

I give too little to these precious flowers.

I would like to be more sensitive to their needs;

More empathetic to their woes;

More enthusiastic in encouragement;

More sincere in conversation;

More selfless in deed.

How can I expect my garden to grow unless I tend carefully to it?
