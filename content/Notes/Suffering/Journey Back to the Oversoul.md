Soul of my soul...

Where are you?

Have I drifted too far away this time?

Wandered too deep into the dark wilderness

Of vanity and falseness?

I won't find you in this place.

But how do I find my way back in the dark?

"Empty and dark I'll raise my lantern..." [[Kahlil Gibran]]

And you will light my way back.

It is through pain that we find [[truth]].

That's when we're low to the ground.

And the highest truths are found at the bottom.

It is through [[suffering]] that we transcend our chains.

They melt off in the fires of affliction.

The same fires that burn away the illusions that keep us from the infinite grandness of life

As it really is...

Relentlessly tragic

Inexhaustibly kind

Heartbreaking

Awe-inspiring

An eternal dance of darkness and light.
