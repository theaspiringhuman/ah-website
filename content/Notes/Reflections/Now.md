All I can do is live and experience now as it unfolds.

Now has so much to say.

Now is the only place to find truth.

It's not in yesterday or tomorrow.

Only today contains the wisdom I need.

It's only in now that you & I connect.

I cannot now inhabit the old me & speak to you then & listen to what you have have said when I wouldn't listen.

I cannot change the past.

I cannot now jump into the skin if the future me & attempt to glean from the experiences that have yet to happen.

I cannot taste of future fruit.

I can only be here now.

& enjoy it.
