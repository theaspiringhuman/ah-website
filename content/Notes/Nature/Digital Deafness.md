They can keep their devices and fancy cars and fast-paced lifestyles, as long as they leave me the mountains and trees and soft songs of birds.

The things they are too busy and important to care for I will cherish all the more that they have become so rarely admired.

These are the secrets of nature... the whispered gems of [[truth]] and [[beauty]] that can only be heard at a silent dawn.

These once so widely revered [[value|treasures]] of life have become obstructed by imposing vanities drawn from a void of absurdity. 

This world is so loud and intrusive. So contrived and manufactured. 

[[Nature]] is always speaking.

But we have been deafened by this digital age.
