I came across this quote in my reading last night (while [[Milan]] rested her sweet little head on my shoulder), & the idea hit me right in the tummy & gave me butterflies. All of this time of shedding [[illusions]], dying to false personas, [[loss]] & [[grief]] & pain & [[heartbreak]]... To know that there is new life on the other side of this is what keeps me going. The only viable way to conceptualize this season has been to see it as a metamorphosis of the soul, and I will emerge from this [[Nigredo - The Blackening|nigredo]] with treasures that would have been impossible to attain in any other way.
