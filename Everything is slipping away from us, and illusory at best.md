"If life is a dream, what does that imply about how we should live? Should we become detached, like the Stoics or Buddhist monks, recognizing that all things pass? Or should we embrace the dream, like lucid dreamers, shaping it with intention and wonder?" - Chat GPT

The question of how to live a life... detached, accepting of suffering, or engaged and "consciously creating" a reality that we desire. Are these mutually exclusive?

---

People who have near death experiences come back saying that the afterlife is more real than this life, and that the clarity of it makes this life seem like a dream. 

Is that why we are so forgetful and vague?
